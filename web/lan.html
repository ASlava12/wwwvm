wwwvm — Virtual LAN · parallel VMs on one L2 switch

Each VM runs in its own Web Worker; their NICs are bridged by an in-page learning switch. Tick Internet to also give every VM a NAT gateway (10.0.2.2 → your WebSocket relay) so they reach each other and the outside world over one NIC. Needs a LAN image (WWWVM_NET_LAN + NIC modules). · single-VM workspace
