wwwvm — Virtual LAN · parallel VMs on one L2 switch

Each VM runs in its own Web Worker; their NICs are bridged by an in-page learning switch (no NAT). Needs a LAN image (built with WWWVM_NET_LAN + NIC modules). Boot, wait for the shells, then e.g. ping -c2 10.0.0.2 in VM 1. · single-VM demo
